Our Mission

Zanzibar's Coffee Shop is dedicated to providing our customers with the best coffees from around the world in an environment that is friendly and inviting. We have travelled the world to learn about the best roasting techniques and partnered with the best people to ensure we are getting top notch beans. We have a relaxing environment that makes for a great place to study or get some work done. We love to see people coming together and sharing their ideas over a cup of fresh brew. Zanzibar's Coffee Shop also provides live music every night so be sure to stop by and check us out! If you are interested in performing, feel free to contact us in person, on the phone, or just fill out our contact form online!
